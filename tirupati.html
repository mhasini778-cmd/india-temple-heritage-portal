Tirupati Balaji Temple

Location

Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, India

Main Deity

Lord Venkateswara (Balaji)

Historical Background

Tirupati Balaji Temple is one of the richest and most visited temples in the world. Located on the Tirumala Hills, it is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.

Darshan Timings

3:00 AM – 11:00 PM

Important Festivals

  • Brahmotsavam
  • Vaikunta Ekadasi
  • Rathasapthami
