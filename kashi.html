Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Location

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India

Main Deity

Lord Shiva

Historical Background

Kashi Vishwanath Temple is one of the most sacred temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is situated on the banks of the River Ganga in Varanasi.

Darshan Timings

3:00 AM – 11:00 PM

Important Festivals

  • Maha Shivaratri
  • Dev Deepawali
  • Kartik Purnima
