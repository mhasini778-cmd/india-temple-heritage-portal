Srisailam Temple

Location

Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh, India

Main Deity

Lord Mallikarjuna Swamy & Goddess Bhramaramba

Historical Background

Srisailam Temple is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and one of the eighteen Shakti Peethas. It is located on the Nallamala Hills.

Darshan Timings

4:30 AM – 10:00 PM

Important Festivals

  • Maha Shivaratri
  • Dasara
  • Karthika Masam
