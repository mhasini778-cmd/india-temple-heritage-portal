Ramanathaswamy Temple

Location

Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India

Main Deity

Lord Shiva

Historical Background

Ramanathaswamy Temple is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites. It is famous for its long corridors and sacred water tanks.

Darshan Timings

5:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Important Festivals

  • Maha Shivaratri
  • Navaratri
  • Arudra Darshan
