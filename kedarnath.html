Kedarnath Temple

Location

Kedarnath, Uttarakhand, India

Main Deity

Lord Shiva

Historical Background

Kedarnath Temple is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas and an important part of the Char Dham pilgrimage.

Darshan Timings

4:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Important Festivals

  • Badri-Kedar Festival
  • Maha Shivaratri
