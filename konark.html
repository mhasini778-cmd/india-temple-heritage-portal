Konark Sun Temple

Location

Konark, Odisha, India

Main Deity

Sun God Surya

Historical Background

Konark Sun Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It was built in the 13th century and is designed like a giant chariot.

Darshan Timings

6:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Important Festivals

  • Konark Dance Festival
  • Chandrabhaga Mela
